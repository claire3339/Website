About Us

How We Began

We started off as a small, family owned ice cream and candy shop back in 1990. We have had a farm, so ice cream was our first thought to start making in our store. Once we got started with that, and started getting customers we decided to start making candy. We all have the biggest sweet tooths, so this was one of our best decisions!
